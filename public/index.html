About IEEE Computer Society

The IEEE Computer Society is the world's premier organization for computer professionals. For over 75 years, we have been advancing the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology.

Our Mission

We foster innovation, education, and collaboration among computing professionals worldwide. Our community spans across industry, academia, and research, driving technological advancement for humanity's benefit.
